The program

Working with native plants can be deeply rewarding experience, and you'll have the library there to help out when you need it.

Learn

Take classes at the library to learn how to start seeds, care for your native plants, and collect your own seeds.

Borrow

Stop by the Kilton Public Library to browse the seeds available for checkout. We have a wide range of plants available all of which are native to the Lebanon, NH area.

Grow

After your seeds have sprouted, it's time to plant. This is a great outdoor activity to do with friends.

Collect

All of your hard work has paid off. You've enjoyed a summer of beautiful flower and now it's time to harvest. Bring back some of your borrowed seeds to the library and we'll lend them out to someone else!
